01
Case Study

Gulf Bitumen Global B2B Lead Generation

Corporate WordPress B2B
View Live Site →
The Challenge

Gulf Bitumen is one of UAE's leading bitumen exporters, supplying to buyers across the Middle East and beyond. Their old digital presence didn't reflect their scale they were losing credibility and inbound leads to competitors who simply had better-looking, better-ranking websites. The task: build something that could hold its own in an international B2B search environment.

What Was Built

A full-scale WordPress corporate website with dedicated pages for every bitumen grade (Penetration, Viscosity, Performance, Cutback, and more), country-specific supply pages for Bahrain, UAE, and Iraq, and a structured content architecture designed to rank for long-tail industrial search terms. The design communicates industrial authority clean, structured, and built for procurement managers, not casual browsers.

02
Case Study

Younique Creations Multi-Brand Shopify Store

E-Commerce Shopify Multi-Brand
View Live Site →
The Challenge

Younique Creations wasn't just one brand it was an umbrella for multiple product lines: jewellery under their main label, birthday decor through Decofy, baking accessories through TrendyTrolley, and bridal and baby shower collections. The challenge was building a single store that could house all of this without feeling cluttered, while making it easy for different types of shoppers to find exactly what they came for.

What Was Built

A structured Shopify store with a clean navigation architecture that separates product categories and brand identities without fragmenting the shopping experience. Each sub-brand has its own collection space while sharing a unified checkout. Product pages were built for mobile-first browsing the primary channel for their target audience with social links integrated to drive traffic from Instagram directly into purchase flow.

Case Study

Essence Elevators Premium Corporate Presence

Corporate WordPress Engineering
View Live Site →
The Challenge

Essence Elevators operates in a market where trust is everything businesses and housing developers don't buy an elevator without confidence in the company behind it. They needed a website that reflected the premium, precision-engineered nature of their products and could hold its own when being evaluated alongside established competitors during a buying decision.

What Was Built

A premium WordPress website with sophisticated scroll-triggered animations, a full product and elevator type showcase, and detailed About and contact sections designed to pass the credibility check during vendor evaluation. The visual language refined, minimal, and confident directly mirrors the quality of the physical product. Built with the Hellix theme architecture for smooth performance across devices.
